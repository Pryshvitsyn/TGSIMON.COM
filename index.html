Why Protect Against Corrosion?

Corrosion is the gradual degradation of metals through chemical reactions with the environment. Without proper protection, assets suffer leaks, structural failure, and expensive downtime. Protective coatings, cathodic protection and routine maintenance extend asset life and ensure safety.

Overview

TGSIMON delivers services for pipelines, storage, marine, utilities and industrial plants. We specialize in Cathodic Protection System design, implementation and management in all variety of assets. Our engineers evaluate corrosion threats, model current flow, select optimal anodes, oversee installation and provide long-term monitoring. The approach integrates coating, inspection and maintenance so infrastructure stays safe, efficient and compliant. We partner with clients to tailor programs that extend service life and cut downtime.
